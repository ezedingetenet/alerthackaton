About the Forum

The Digital Health & Innovation Forum is Ethiopia's premier gathering for healthcare transformation. We bring together three distinct communities — healthcare professionals, technology innovators, and industry leaders — to collaborate on solutions that matter.

This is not just another conference. It's a platform for authentic dialogue, practical innovation, and meaningful partnerships that drive real change in healthcare delivery.

Whether you're a doctor exploring digital tools, a startup building health tech solutions, or an industry partner seeking collaboration opportunities, the forum provides the space and connections you need.
