The Loom Story

Unveiling a rich, meticulous dialogue between ancient Addis Ababa loom weavers and modern structured Savile Row tailoring.

Centuries of Weaving

THE SACRED TIBEB EMBROIDERY.

The Tibeb pattern is a geometric tapestry traditionally hand-woven into cotton shawls (Gabis) to denote noble lineage and spiritual wisdom.

At Habesha Thread, our master artisans spin organic Ethiopian cotton onto ancient wooden looms, introducing premium gold silk threads directly into tailored blazer sleeves, suit jackets, and luxury waistcoats.
